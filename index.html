About Nature

Nature (in a broad sense has a meaning equivalent to the natural world, the physical world, or the material world) refers to the phenomena of the physical world as well as life in general. The scale of nature extends from the subatomic to the cosmic. The study of nature is a big part of science. Although humans are part of nature, human activities are often understood as a separate category from other natural phenomena
